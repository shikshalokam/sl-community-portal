ShikshaLokam enables and amplifies leadership development opportunities for individuals and institutions engaged in K-12 education systems

As a platform for education leadership, ShikshaLokam offers open-source technological capabilities and learning resources to a variety of ecosystem actors, including Individuals, NGOs, and Government. ShikshaLokam aims to do so in ways that support education leadership development at a large scale with high impact in a sustainable manner.
